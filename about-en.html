About Us

PitBikeDrive is more than just a club — it’s a community of true adrenaline lovers!

We unite people who crave freedom, emotions, and new experiences. Our pitbike tours give you a chance to feel like a real racer, test your skills, and share excitement with friends.

We provide fully serviced bikes, protective gear, and thorough briefings. Whether you're a beginner or experienced rider, we’ve got you covered. All routes are designed for both thrill and safety.

Our mission is to promote pitbike culture in Ukraine and make it accessible to everyone.

Ride. Feel. Live it — PitBikeDrive.
